OUR STORY

Book Club opened in July 2020 and serves as a community hub of the Upper West Side: a new kind of independent bookstore featuring top-notch coffee, wine, and gin. This spirited and dynamic neighborhood is reflected in the store: from the cozy living room-feel decor, to curated book inventory, locally roasted coffee, and NY state wine. Come hang out with us!

Red Queen
Victoria Aveyard is the #1 New York Times bestselling author of Red Queen and Glass Sword. Victoria was born and raised in East Longmeadow, Massachusetts, a small town known only for the worst traffic rotary in the continental United States. She moved to Los Angeles to earn a BFA in screenwriting at USC. She currently splits her time between the East and West coasts. As an author and screenwriter, she uses her career as an excluse to read too many books and watch too many movies.
